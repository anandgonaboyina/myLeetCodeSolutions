<h2><a href="https://takeuforward.org/plus/">Best time to buy and sell stock</a></h2><h3>Medium</h3><hr><p>Given an array arr of n integers, where arr[i] represents price of the stock on the ith day.&nbsp;Determine the<strong> maximum profit</strong> achievable by buying and selling the stock at most once.&nbsp;</p><p><br></p><p>The stock should be purchased before selling it, and both actions cannot occur on the same day.</p><br><br><p class="tuf-text-14">Example 1</p><div class="tuf-example text-new_secondary"><p><strong>Input:</strong> arr = [10, 7, 5, 8, 11, 9]</p><p><strong>Output:</strong> 6</p><p><strong>Explanation:</strong> Buy on day 3 (price = 5) and sell on day 5 (price = 11), profit = 11 - 5 = 6.</p></div><br><br><p class="tuf-text-14 ">Example 2</p><div class="tuf-example text-new_secondary"><p><strong>Input:</strong>&nbsp;arr = [5, 4, 3, 2, 1]</p><p><strong>Output:</strong> 0</p><p><strong>Explanation:</strong> In this case, no transactions are made. Therefore, the maximum profit remains 0.</p></div><br><br><p class="tuf-text-14 ">Constraints</p><div class="tuf-dark-content-box"><ul><li>1 &lt;= n&lt;= 10<sup>5</sup></li><li>0 &lt;= arr[i] &lt;= 10<sup>6</sup></li></ul></div><br><br>
